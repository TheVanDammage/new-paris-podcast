Your host

Lindsey Tramuta

Lindsey Tramuta is an American author and journalist based in Paris. She is the author of The New Paris and The New Parisienne, and writes for The New York Times, Condé Nast Traveler, AFAR, and more.

Since 2017, her podcast has served as a companion to her writing — a place for extended conversations with the creators, entrepreneurs, and thinkers who are actively transforming what it means to live in Paris today.
