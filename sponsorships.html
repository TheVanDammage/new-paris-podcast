Why sponsor

An Audience That Trusts the Voice

The New Paris Podcast has built an audience over nine years through depth, consistency, and genuine editorial independence. Listeners don't just tune in — they follow Lindsey's recommendations, buy her books, and visit the places and people she covers.

Sponsoring this podcast means your brand is introduced personally by a trusted host to an audience that is pre-qualified: curious, educated, and highly engaged with Paris and French culture, travel, food, design, and ideas.

Host-read ads perform significantly above average on this show. Because Lindsey speaks to her audience directly and authentically, sponsor mentions feel like genuine recommendations — not interruptions.

Who you reach

The Audience Profile

Listeners are predominantly English-speaking, based in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia, with a significant expat and Francophile community in France. They skew 28–55, are highly educated, and have disposable income directed toward travel, culture, and quality goods.

They include travelers planning trips to Paris, expats living in France, students of French language and culture, professionals in creative and international fields, and readers of quality journalism and literary nonfiction.

This is not a mass-market audience — it is a niche, loyal, and highly convertible one.
